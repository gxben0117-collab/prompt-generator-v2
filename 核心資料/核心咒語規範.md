MANDATORY FIRST STEP:
Check the current ChatGPT message / conversation for an uploaded reference photo of the person.
If no reference photo is attached or visible, STOP and ask the user to upload the person's photo first.
Do not generate an image, do not invent a face, and do not proceed from text alone.

## 固定規定 (CANONICAL FIXED PROMPT RULES)

### 真人身份優先｜最高權重

生成前先讀取上傳照片中的真實臉部身份與人體比例。

所有奇幻風格、服裝、妝容、場景設計，都只能建立在原始真人身份之上。

不可重新設計一張新的臉。

AI 必須先建立：真人身份、真實人體比例、真實攝影結構，其次才建立奇幻風格。

人物主體權重高於場景特效權重。

優先呈現真人攝影感，而不是 AI 概念藝術感。

不可因為奇幻風格而重新設計真人五官比例。

保留上傳照片中的完整臉部身份與五官。

不可改變：臉型、眼型、鼻型、嘴型、下顎線、五官比例、可辨識特徵。

保留自然臉部不對稱、真實年齡細節、真人感。

若奇幻、服裝、妝容、電影感與臉部身份衝突，一律保留真人臉部身份。

避免：AI 美女臉、網紅濾鏡感、娃娃臉比例、模板美女臉、標準化五官。

### 真實人體骨架

優先建立真實成年女性骨架。

頭部大小必須與肩膀、身體維持真實人體比例。

保持：平衡肩寬、真實鎖骨、自然胸腔厚度、正常軀幹深度、自然脖子連接。

禁止：大頭比例、壓縮上半身、過窄肩膀、娃娃比例、頭重腳輕、身體過小、動漫骨架、卡通化人體。

人物身體必須與真實臉部自然融合。

### 電影攝影結構

固定使用：50mm 全片幅電影攝影視角。

真實人像拍攝距離。全身完整入鏡。電影感中遠景構圖。

人物與場景必須保持真實空間透視。

避免：人像特寫、自拍視角、誇張透視、半身近拍、腰上人像、臉部放大、beauty framing。

避免臉部主導畫面比例。優先呈現完整人物與環境關係。

### 真實攝影感

畫面必須像「真實攝影師拍攝的真人奇幻電影海報」，而不是 AI 插畫。

強調：真實光影、真實空氣感、真實環境互動、真實皮膚反光、真實布料重量、真實人體站姿。

奇幻世界只能襯托真人，不能取代真人。

人物必須是被拍進奇幻世界的真人，不是重新設計的奇幻女主角。

真人演員被拍進奇幻世界。奇幻世界支撐真人照片身份，而不是重新設計奇幻角色。

### 真實人類皮膚質感

保留真實人類皮膚質感。

保留：自然毛孔、自然皮膚紋理、自然皮膚反光、自然膚色變化、自然臉部微瑕疵、自然皮膚陰影。

皮膚需像：「真實攝影師拍攝的真人皮膚」

而不是：AI 磨皮皮膚、CG 遊戲皮膚、蠟像皮膚、奶油肌、塑膠皮膚。

允許：自然皮膚細節、真實電影光影下的皮膚紋理、自然膚色層次、真實攝影皮膚反射。

避免：過度磨皮、過度柔焦、AI 美肌、假高光、不自然發光皮膚、過度乾淨皮膚、AI 假人皮膚感。

real human skin texture, natural pores, subtle skin imperfections, realistic cinematic skin shading, natural complexion variation, photographed human skin realism, non-airbrushed skin texture.

### 動作控制

電影海報式站姿。自然人體重心。肩膀與軀幹平衡。手部比例正確。腿部比例正確。自然手指結構。

姿勢需配合臉部角度自然生成。

避免：扭曲四肢、錯誤手指、不合理肢體、過度誇張動作、不自然姿勢、跳躍、旋轉、背對鏡頭、手遮臉、手舉過頭。

### 固定品質描述

8K 超高細節。電影級光影。真實皮膚紋理。高細節布料物理。超高畫質。documentary photo realism。

### 固定負面詞

禁止：AI 美女臉、美肌濾鏡感、塑膠皮膚、過度磨皮、大頭比例、人像特寫臉部放大、自拍視角、娃娃比例、卡通化人體、動漫骨架、肩膀過窄、身體過小、錯誤四肢、扭曲手指、不自然動作、AI 插畫感、CG 遊戲角色感、AI 時尚大片感、fashion editorial、magazine cover、beauty campaign、網美風、角色卡面風格、臉部主導畫面、模板美女臉、標準化五官、過度 HDR、塑膠感 cinematic realism。

## IDENTITY & EXPRESSION PRESERVATION (CRITICAL)
* Use the uploaded photo as the only identity reference.
* Preserve exact facial geometry, facial proportions, eye shape, nose structure, mouth structure, skin identity, natural expression behavior, emotional characteristics, and recognizable likeness.
* Keep the same person recognizable across hairstyle, styling, makeup, costume, lighting, emotional, environmental, and photographic variations.
* Preserve the uploaded person's natural eye emotion, mouth tension, smile structure, expression behavior, and emotional characteristics while allowing scene-appropriate emotional variation.
* Apply makeup as surface cosmetics only. Makeup may affect color, texture, lighting polish, and stylistic mood, but must never reshape identity or alter the person's natural facial structure.

## IDENTITY SOVEREIGNTY PROMPT RULE (CRITICAL)
* The uploaded face identity has higher priority than fantasy styling, cinematic aesthetics, beauty enhancement, costume elegance, genre atmosphere, role title, or editorial presentation.
* Do not beautify, idealize, redesign, reinterpret, reconstruct, stylize, glamour-enhance, or replace the face.
* Preserve the exact real-person facial structure from the uploaded photo: original forehead shape, eye spacing, eyelid structure, nose geometry, cheek volume, jaw width, lip shape, natural asymmetry, natural age detail, and authentic skin texture.
* The result must look like a documentary-real photograph of this exact uploaded person inside a designed world, not a generated fantasy character based on the reference photo.

## FANTASY SCOPE LIMITATION
* Fantasy styling may affect environment, costume, props, lighting, atmosphere, color palette, and background world-building only.
* Fantasy styling must not affect facial geometry, eye shape, nose shape, mouth structure, jawline, cheek volume, skin age detail, or recognizable likeness.
* Card titles may keep world-building names such as succubus, queen, goddess, or demon, but prompt character context must describe the uploaded real person inside that world.

## ANTI-BEAUTY-TEMPLATE OVERRIDE
* Forbid influencer-face reconstruction, fantasy queen face template, actress-face conversion, xianxia heroine face, doll-like symmetry, V-shape jaw, sharpened chin, enlarged eyes, ultra-smooth skin, cinematic beauty reconstruction, AI glamour-face replacement, and commercial retouching that changes identity.
* Beauty words must be downgraded to real-person photographic words. Use authentic skin texture, natural asymmetry, real-person appearance, documentary portrait realism, and reference face fidelity.
* Makeup must be surface-only: color and texture only, no contouring that changes cheekbone or jawline, no eye-shape enhancement, no lip reshaping, no facial restructuring cosmetics.

## STYLE CONTAMINATION BLACKLIST
* High-risk beauty words: `beauty`, `gorgeous`, `glamorous`, `perfect skin`, `flawless skin`, `porcelain skin`, `model face`, `influencer face`.
* High-risk archetype words: `succubus queen`, `demon queen`, `fantasy queen`, `fantasy empress`, `goddess face`, `goddess aura`, `immortal aesthetic`, `xianxia heroine`, `dark fantasy heroine`.
* High-risk editorial words: `magazine cover`, `editorial realism`, `premium fantasy editorial realism`, `character editorial realism`, `concept art quality`, `character concept art`, `fashion beauty portrait`.
* High-risk angle words: `looking upward`, `dramatic side profile`, `over-the-shoulder`, `low-angle hero shot`, `forced-perspective hero shot`.
* High-risk head-scale words: `half-body close portrait`, `waist-up portrait`, `face-readable half-body`, `face-readable close framing`, `upper costume details`, `beauty-camera framing`.

## TIERED IDENTITY-SAFE SANITIZE PIPELINE
All prompt fields generated from presets, UI options, user extra text, and style examples must pass a 10-tier sanitize pipeline before final output.

1. `tier01_identity_sovereignty`: remove or rewrite identity replacement language such as template face, actress-face conversion, influencer-face reconstruction, AI glamour-face replacement, and standardized facial features.
2. `tier02_beauty_template`: downgrade beauty-template words such as beautiful woman, attractive face, pretty face, celestial beauty, immortal beauty, perfect skin, flawless skin, porcelain skin, gorgeous, and glamorous.
3. `tier03_editorial_commercial`: downgrade magazine, editorial, premium, high-end, luxury beauty, camera-ready, beauty campaign, and photographic polish language into documentary real-person language.
4. `tier04_fantasy_archetype`: contain fantasy archetypes so succubus, demon queen, fantasy queen, goddess, heroine, fairy, elf, and immortal wording affects environment, costume, props, and atmosphere only.
5. `tier05_makeup_restructure`: force makeup into surface-only cosmetics. Rewrite glow makeup, luminous eye shimmer, cat-eye, fox-eye, contouring, heavy eyeliner, defined lashes, and reshaping makeup.
6. `tier06_camera_angle_identity`: rewrite high-drift angles such as looking upward, side profile, over-the-shoulder, low-angle hero shot, and forced perspective into stable camera-level face-readable alternatives.
7. `tier07_head_body_scale`: rewrite close-up beauty framing and head-dominant portrait phrases into balanced medium, three-quarter, or full-body real-person framing.
8. `tier08_quality_retouch`: downgrade HDR, ultra realistic, cinematic realism, professional photoshoot, vivid advertising color, and over-polished quality terms into natural dynamic range and documentary realism.
9. `tier09_motion_obstruction`: prevent poses that obscure or destabilize the face: jumping, spinning, back-facing, arms overhead, covering face, face-hidden pose, and mannequin pose.
10. `tier10_scope_language`: keep worldbuilding vivid while restricting risky style words to external scope. Fantasy, queen, goddess, cinematic, fashion, and light effects may describe scene, costume, props, atmosphere, and color, never facial structure.

The order matters. Identity sovereignty and anti-beauty downgrade must run before style, camera, makeup, and quality language.

## PROMPT SCOPE RULES
* Face-internal scope: facial geometry, eyes, eyelids, nose, mouth, lips, jawline, cheek volume, forehead, age detail, skin texture, and recognizable likeness. These fields are preserve-only.
* Face-safe makeup scope: surface color, texture, and mood only. No contouring, no eye-shape change, no lash enhancement that changes eye identity, no lip reshaping, no jaw or cheekbone reconstruction.
* Face-external scope: environment, costume, props, lighting, atmosphere, architecture, weather, color palette, action, and camera distance.
* If a word can affect both face and environment, treat it as environment-only. Example: `goddess` may describe temple scale, costume materials, or mythic light; it must not describe the person's face.
* If a prompt phrase makes the person "become" a fantasy archetype, rewrite it as the uploaded real person photographed inside that archetype's world.

## IDENTITY-SAFE REPLACEMENT PRINCIPLE
* `fantasy beauty` -> `real-person portrait in a fantasy environment`.
* `goddess aura` -> `mythic atmosphere around the environment`.
* `magazine cover` -> `natural environmental portrait photography`.
* `editorial realism` -> `natural cinematic realism`.
* `concept art quality` -> `real-person photographic quality`.
* `looking upward` -> `camera-level gaze with stable face orientation`.
* `half-body portrait from waist up` -> `medium three-quarter portrait with balanced head-to-shoulder-to-torso proportion`.

## IDENTITY ELASTICITY (REALISM SUPPORT)
* Allow natural hairstyle variation, hair movement, cinematic framing, realistic environmental interaction, candid energy, emotional variation, and realistic photographic imperfection.
* Realistic human variation is encouraged. The subject should feel alive, naturally photographed, and contextually integrated into each scene rather than frozen into a static presentation template.
* Natural cinematic imperfection is allowed, including realistic wind movement, partial framing, soft environmental obstruction, dynamic posing, candid behavior, and realistic photographic asymmetry.

## PROMPT GENERATION RESTRAINTS (ANTI-AI ARTIFACTS)
* Avoid identity drift, influencer-template faces, plastic skin, distorted anatomy, excessive beauty filtering, mannequin-like posing, artificial symmetry, frozen expressions, repetitive framing, and generic AI beauty aesthetics.
* Maintain realistic anatomy, believable posing, authentic emotional presence, realistic lighting response, natural skin texture, realistic depth, and photographic realism.
* Preserve realistic human texture and believable photographic imperfections rather than overprocessed AI beauty rendering.

## ANATOMY & VISUAL FRAME
* Maintain realistic adult anatomy with coherent joints, correct finger count, natural limb proportions, believable body balance, and physically plausible pose flow.
* Important facial identity features should remain readable and recognizable.
* Natural hair movement, cinematic shadowing, environmental interaction, partial framing, and realistic scene obstruction are allowed as long as the uploaded person remains recognizable.
* Role, beauty, luxury, bridal, celebrity, goddess, editorial, model, fantasy, or cinematic keywords describe styling, costume, lighting, mood, and artistic direction only. They must never replace the uploaded person's identity or recognizable appearance.
* Head must not dominate the frame. Maintain balanced head-to-shoulder-to-torso proportion and enough camera distance to keep body scale natural.
* Avoid close-up portrait compression, beauty-camera framing, low-angle hero perspective, and any pose that requires inventing a new unsupported facial angle.

## PROMPT STRUCTURING & STYLE
* Compose prompts in the following flow:
identity priority → anti-beauty-template override → anatomy and head-body lock → environment → lighting → real-person subject context → surface-only makeup → outfit → identity-safe action → balanced camera → real-person photographic finish
* Prioritize realistic camera behavior, cinematic environmental depth, believable body alignment, authentic emotional presence, and natural subject integration within the scene.
* Support a wide range of realistic photographic aesthetics including candid, documentary, lifestyle, travel, film photography, natural light photography, and spontaneous realistic photography styles.
* Cinematic, editorial, fashion, premium, luxury, goddess, queen, fairy, anime, or game words are allowed only when they remain face-external and pass the sanitize pipeline.

## VISUAL REALISM PRIORITY
* The final image should feel like a believable real photograph of the uploaded person captured in a natural moment rather than a synthetic AI-generated beauty render.
* Avoid overly rigid symmetry, mannequin-like posing, excessive glamour filtering, artificial skin smoothing, or repetitive static composition.

## USER INPUT FIELD RULES (使用者輸入欄位規則)

### 主題｜theme

使用者必填。

輸出格式：`主題：...`

UI 提醒：只填角色、職業、人物名字、劇情狀況。

避免描述：長相、美貌、氣質、年齡感、女神感。否則可能導致 AI 換臉。

禁止輸入：美女、絕美、女神、仙氣、網紅感、完美五官、年輕少女感、白幼瘦、精靈系美女、高級臉、明星臉、模特臉、純欲風、初戀感、完美肌膚、零毛孔、巴掌臉、漫畫腿、大眼娃娃、性感尤物、夢幻美人、國色天香、flawless beauty、perfect face、beautiful woman、goddess、porcelain skin、fashion model、instagram model、beauty editorial、cinematic beauty、magazine cover。

正確範例：`三國江南小喬`、`月下彈琴的江南貴族女子`。

不建議範例：`絕世美女小喬`、`仙氣女神`、`傾國傾城`。

### 場景｜scene

若完全沒有內容，核心預設為：使用配合主題的電影場景。動作配合主題。若主題無動作提示，則由 AI 設計合乎角色風格的動作。

欄位用途：人在做什麼、在哪裡、做什麼事情、環境背景、特效元素。

場景元素必須支援人物存在感。不可讓場景特效主導畫面。人物主體優先於奇幻特效與環境細節。

避免：大量發光特效、漂浮物、粒子暴風、魔法爆炸、過量光效。

### 服裝｜costume

若沒有內容，核心預設為：配合主題，若主題無服飾提示，讓 AI 設計服裝裝飾要有華麗感，但仍保有真實攝影感。

欄位用途：衣服、材質、風格、吊飾、首飾、頭飾。

### 妝容｜makeup

固定限制：在不改變原始臉部結構前提下，只限表面妝容效果。不可改變骨相。不可 AI 美顏化。

若沒有內容，核心預設為：配合主題讓 AI 設計妝容與髮型。

欄位用途：上妝畫法、頭髮髮型。

髮型只能改變頭髮造型。

不可改變：頭骨輪廓、額頭比例、臉型輪廓、髮際線結構。

### 動作與道具｜action

有內容時才輸出：`【動作與道具】...`

用途：角色互動、持有物件、武器、樂器、動作姿態。

### 構圖｜composition

若有內容，輸出：`角色卡構圖：...`

固定構圖規定：人物整體存在感優先於臉部特寫。臉部不可過度銳化。避免時尚雜誌封面感。避免臉部主導畫面。優先呈現完整人物與環境關係。

### 尺寸比例｜ratio

可選：1:1 正方形、2:3 直向、3:4 直向人像、4:3 橫向、9:16 手機螢幕、2.39:1 電影橫幅。

預設：9:16 手機螢幕。

全身完整入鏡。使用中遠景真人攝影距離。避免自拍感與近距離人像特寫。避免臉部主導畫面比例。

### 身材設定欄位規則 (Cup Size Field Rules)

#### cupSize 欄位說明
角色卡可選擇性添加 `cupSize` 欄位來指定身材罩杯大小。

**欄位格式：**
- 欄位名稱：`cupSize`
- 值類型：字串（必須包含英文字母或數字）
- 範例值：`"F"`、`"G"`、`"D"`、`"DD"` 等

**使用規則：**
1. 如果角色卡包含 `cupSize` 欄位且值包含英文或數字，系統會在固定咒語中加入對應的罩杯設定
2. 生成的咒語會包含完整的身材描述規範，包括：
   - 維持真實成熟成年女性體積感
   - 自然豐滿胸型需建立在真實胸腔骨架結構之上
   - 保持自然胸部重量與真實軀幹厚度
   - 罩杯預設[指定值]
   - 避免動漫式誇張胸型、極端沙漏型身材等
3. 如果角色卡沒有 `cupSize` 欄位，則使用標準身體結構描述（不含罩杯設定）

**已設定系列：**
以下系列的所有角色卡已預設 `cupSize: "F"`：

- 墮天使系列（4個角色卡）
- 女王系列（12個角色卡）

**技術實現：**
- 位置：`core.js` 中的 `buildPrompt()` 函數
- 判斷邏輯：優先檢查 `styleData.cupSize` 欄位，若存在且符合格式則使用 `buildBodyStructureWithCupSize()` 函數生成帶罩杯設定的身體結構描述
- 向後兼容：若無 `cupSize` 欄位，魅魔系列仍使用預設 F 罩杯（向後兼容舊版邏輯）
